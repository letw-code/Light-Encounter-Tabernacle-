Our Mission

Empowering Young Hearts

Drawing from commitment to evangelizing and discipling children, LETW's focus on encouraging parents and edifying servants, and Tabernacle's profound worship experiences, Light Encounter Tabernacle Kids Ministry serves children ages 2-17. We create transformative spaces where kids value God's Word through worship, lessons, games, and one-on-one guidance.

Our Promise: A safe, nurturing environment where children encounter God's love, build meaningful friendships, develop strong faith foundations, and discover their unique gifts—just like the joyful camps and outreaches that inspire us.

We blend engaging worship inspired by Tabernacle traditions, biblical teaching from deeper revelation's Search-the-Scriptures, and community service from LETW's outreach to help children understand their purpose in God's plan.
